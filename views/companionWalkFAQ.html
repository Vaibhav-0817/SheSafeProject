Companion Walk FAQs

1: What is the Companion Walk feature?

Answer: The Companion Walk feature allows you to request a virtual safety escort through potentially risky areas on campus. It provides an added layer of security when you need to traverse areas you may feel uncomfortable in.

2: How do I request a Companion Walk?

Answer: To request a Companion Walk, log in to your account, go to the "Safety Services" section, and select "Request Virtual Walk." Fill out the details, including your destination and expected arrival time. Once submitted, your request will be sent to available safety escorts.

3: Who are the safety escorts, and how are they selected?

Answer: Safety escorts are volunteers from the campus community who have expressed their willingness to assist fellow students and faculty. They are selected based on their availability and proximity to your requested route.

4: Is the Companion Walk service available 24/7?

Answer: Yes, the Companion Walk service is available around the clock to ensure your safety whenever you need it, whether it's during the day or late at night.

5: Is my personal information shared when I request a Companion Walk?

Answer: Your personal information is kept confidential. Only essential details, such as your location and destination, are shared with the safety escort to facilitate the walk. We prioritize your privacy and safety.

6: What if my Companion Walk request is not accepted immediately?

Answer: If your request isn't accepted right away, don't worry. The system will keep trying to find an available safety escort for you. You can also explore other safety options or wait for the request to be accepted, depending on your comfort level and urgency.
